Customer Satisfaction

Our customer’s satisfaction is one of our main priorities here at Xtreme RC. We understand that there are many different types of RC products and combinations for custom builds. That is why we will go above and beyond to find out what would be the best product for your interests. Not only do we want you to leave happy, we also want to exceed your expectations. Please do not hesitate to contact us with any questions or concerns you may have!

Repairs

Just like their full-size counterparts, RC vehicles may require occasional repairs and maintenance. RC cars that race frequently may be more prone to collisions that require repairs afterwards. We have a large inventory of parts to go along with all the RC products we carry. Whether you are looking for a part to fix your RC car or looking for parts to upgrade your car, we likely have it! If we don’t happen to have the part(s) needed to repair your RC car, we will order it in to make sure your car gets back into the action.

Repairs are also offered for all the products we carry, not just RC cars. We even offer repairs to specific model RCs and drones that we may not carry.
