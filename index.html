Welcome to Xtreme RC - Family Owned & Operated Since 2011

Xtreme RC is Charleston’s #1 RC hobby store! We carry a wide variety of remote control products including cars, motorcycles, boats, planes, helicopters, drones, parts, and accessories. We also carry non-RC products such as hoverboards, electronic longboards, quads, and more!

Our RC cars range from beginner cars to advanced cars, custom builds, pre-owned, and clearance. We even buy back used cars and also allow drivers to trade in their car to put towards an upgrade! Interested in an RC product that we don’t carry? Ask us about the product and we may be able to order in the product for you.

Race Track

Xtreme RC also has a dirt race track for racing and for any drivers interested in driving on a track. Want to try out our track but don’t have an RC car to race? No Problem! Whether you want to try out RC car racing before you buy or just want to race with a bunch of friends, Xtreme RC offers RC car rental options to use for race time on the track! For more information on track time and races, Click Here to go to our Events page.

Birthday Parties and Corporate Events

Host your child’s birthday party at Xtreme RC for the most Xtreme birthday party in town! We also specialize in corporate events - bring the office down to us or we’ll come to you! For more information on birthday parties or corporate events, Click Here to go to our Events page.
