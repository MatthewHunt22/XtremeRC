Race Track

Our outdoor race track is a dirt track full of twists, turns, and ramps that is open to any interested drivers 7 days a week! Races are held every Wednesday and Sunday, as well as every other Saturday (weather permitting). Drivers can also come down to use the track for a flat daily fee of $5 per driver.

Local Events

Monck’s Corner Raceway is another popular RC track in the low country. Races are held every other Saturday opposite of our Saturday race schedule. For more information regarding Monck’s Corner Raceway, Click Here to visit their Facebook Group and here to visit their website. RCsignup.com also has a list of all the RC tracks throughout South Carolina, as well as a list of tracks across the country.

National Events

RC Racing can be a big and competitive hobby. There are many major racing events held all across the country, such as the U.S. Open Fuel Championships in Texas or the Custom Works Dixie Nationals in Myrtle Beach, SC. For more information on national events, Click Here.

Birthday Parties

Interested in hosting your child’s birthday party at Xtreme RC? Contact us! Xtreme RC will provide a one of a kind experience for your child’s birthday and his/her friends. We offer a variety of RC cars to race on the dirt track and/or track in the parking lot.

Corporate Events

Whether you’re interested in rewarding the hard work of your employees or just getting out of the office, Xtreme RC provides a great break from work. We’ll even bring the cars and track to you! Xtreme RC will set up a race track in the parking lot of your workplace for you and your employees to race. Contact us for more information!
