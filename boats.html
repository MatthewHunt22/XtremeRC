Boats

Take advantage of Charleston’s surrounding waters with Xtreme RC’s remote control boats! From the pool in your backyard to the ponds, creeks, and lakes across the low country, RC boats are great water entertainment for all!

Product Spotlight

Avanti Thunder Tiger

The Avanti Thunder Tiger is an electric racing boat full of power perfromance. It has a sleek V-shaped hull which gives it an elegant aerodynamic design that knifes through the water.

Specifications

  • Model Number: TTR5129-F11L
  • Length: 29.1 inches
  • Height: 7.1 inches
  • Beam (Width): 8.3 inches
  • Weight: 2.7 lbs
  • ABS blow-molded hull with quick-remove hatch cover
  • Water cooled OBL 29/19-15M & controller BLC-40M installed
  • Factory assembled and Ready-To-Run

    • Kyosho RC Surfer

    The Kyosho RC Surfer takes a different approach to RC boats, making your normal trip to the beach or lake a unique experience. This RC “boat” is actually an electric surfboard topped by a surfer dude fit to ride the waves of the water! Kyosho continues to expand beyond conventional RC and joins forces with Lost, the largest surfboard manufacturer in the United States, to create an RC surfboard with real Lost surfboard graphics.

    Specifications

  • Scale: 1/5
  • Length: 26 inches
  • Width: 6 inches
  • Height: 13 inches
  • Radio: 2.4GHz Syncro KT-231P
  • Motor: Kyosho G24 Motor (24T)
  • Unique rudder design protects prop in shallow waters
